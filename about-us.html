About

NammaMess

NammaMess is an online food delivery application created by a group of engineers who personally experienced the challenges of maintaining regular meal schedules due to their work commitments. Recognizing that this issue is not unique to them, but also affects many others, they have developed a convenient solution. Our platform offers a pre-paid subscription plan that provides customers with three daily meals: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner, ensuring a consistent and hassle-free dining experience throughout the month.
